The Super Ballon d'Or was awarded to Di Stefano in 1989, a prize given to the best football player of the previous three decades. multiple-time Ballon d'Or winners were only eligible and the jury at France Football awarded it to the Spanish-Argentine forward. For years, the trophy was marvelled at in the Real Madrid Museum at the Santiago Bernabeu Stadium, until 2021, when his children auctioned off much of his memorabilia. It was sold to an anonymoud buyer for Â£187,500 and its current location is unknown. there is a possibility that a second 'Super Ballon d'Or' would be awarded in 2029 to award the best player of the previous four decades, with Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo the favourites to win such a prize.
